About us

TEDBUS is a Ghana brand that offers intercity bus service in Ghana.
TEDBUS is an online bus ticketing platform providing ticket booking facility through its website. Headquartered in Kumasi Ghana, it connects bus travelers with a network of over 2500 bus opertors across Ghana. TEDBUS currently runs in Kumasi and Accra and has so far registered over 50 trips.
