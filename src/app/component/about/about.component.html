Our story

TEDBUS is a Ghana brand that offers intercity bus service in Ghana. It is owned by Eric Heckford Bryan and Henry Asiedu.
TEDBUS is an online bus ticketing platform providing ticket booking facility through its website, iOS and Android mobile apps. Headquartered in Kumasi Ghana, it connects bus travelers with a network of over 2500 bus opertors across Ghana. The platform is also a part of Ghana's leading online travel company MakeMyTrip Limited. TEDBUS currently runs in Kumasi and Accra and has so far registered over 50 trips. TEDBUS does not own any bus and does not employ drivers. Its services run in cooperation with regional bus companies. Local partners are responsible for the day-to-day running of routes, while TEDBUS is responsible for permits, network planning, marketing, pricing, remitting the remainfer to its operating partners. This business model is highly scalable and has enables the company to grow quickly. TEDBUS emphasizes the sustainability of its buses, lower prices and free WiFi on board. Customers can choose to offset their carbon footprint when buying a ticket online. The offset costs between 1 - 3% of the ticket price and goes to internationally certified climate projects. TEDBUS has routes served buy electric buses made by BYD Auto and Yutong in Ghana.
